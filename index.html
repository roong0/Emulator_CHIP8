EMULATOR for CHIP8

The computers which originally used Chip-8 language had typically had a 16-key hexadecimal keypad. This has been mapped to a normal keyboard with keys: 1234qwerasdfzxcv

Controls:

  • Space-Invaders

    • W:Start/Play/Shoot Q/E:Left/Right

  • Connect 4 (2 Player)

    • W:Place Q/E:Left/Right

  • Pong

    • 1/Q:Up/Down

  • Brick

    • Q/E:Left/Right

  • Tetris

    • Q:Rotate A:Down W/E:Left/Right
